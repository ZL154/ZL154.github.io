The plugin suite

🔐 JellyfinSecurity

Hardens Jellyfin sign-in: native two-factor auth (TOTP & email), passkeys / WebAuthn, OIDC / SSO (Google, Authentik, Authelia, Keycloak…), brute-force IP banning and impossible-travel detection.

2FA / TOTPPasskeysOIDC / SSOIP banning

⭐ StarTrack

Letterboxd-style star ratings inside Jellyfin, with two-way sync to Trakt, Simkl, Letterboxd and Yamtrack — plus watched/liked sync and a one-time backfill of your existing ratings.

RatingsTraktSimklLetterboxd
GitHub Latest release
📷 screenshot — assets/startrack.png

🏅 AchievementBadges

Earn achievement-style badges from your viewing (and reading) activity — first watches, binges, late-night sessions and more, with a backfill of your existing history.

GamificationAchievementsWatch & read

📺 MissingEpisodes

Scans your library (and Sonarr) for missing episodes and can auto-search for them — so gaps in your TV shows don't go unnoticed.

LibrarySonarrTV shows

Folder-based prerolls before movies and episodes — schedules, rules and maturity gating, for a little cinema magic before the feature starts.
